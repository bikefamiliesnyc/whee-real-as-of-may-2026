Meet the team behind Whee!

Our mission: Get more families on bikes.

Ed Casabian

Favorite Bike: Tern GSD

Ed is an operations leader who as an early Uber employee launched the NYC market. He started Climate Draft to create a pathway for skilled talent to apply their skills to meaningful climate companies. He transports his 2 kids on a cargo bike everyday.

Evan Abel

Favorite Bike: Surly Straggler

Evan is a mechanical engineer and product designer who has launched products at OXO, Doona, and Wim. He led New Verticals & Sustainability at The Rounds and launched Walmart InHome. He founded Friends of Rockaway after Superstorm Sandy. He has bike-packed across eastern Europe and Quebec.
